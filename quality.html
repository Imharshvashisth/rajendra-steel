Testing Facilities

Rigorous Testing Methods

Every batch undergoes strenuous testing in our in-house laboratory to certify mechanical integrity, chemical stability, and structural flawless performance.

Chemical Analysis

Spectrometer testing ensures exact alloy composition and verifies material grades like 304, 316L, and higher alloys.

Mechanical Testing

Tensile, yield strength, and elongation tests are performed to ensure the tubes withstand extreme structural loads.

Non-Destructive

Eddy Current and Hydrostatic testing methods guarantee 100% leak-proof and flawless weld seams.

Dimensional Check

Laser-guided micrometers ensure strict adherence to OD, ID, and thickness tolerances specified by clients.
